Our Story

More Than a Meal

Founded in 2009 by Chef Marco Alvares, Ember & Oak began as a small wood-fired kitchen with a simple belief: extraordinary ingredients, prepared with respect and fire, need nothing else.

Today we remain committed to that founding philosophy — sourcing only from local farms we trust, crafting wine lists that surprise, and creating a dining room where every guest feels like family.
